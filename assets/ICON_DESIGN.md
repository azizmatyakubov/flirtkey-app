- **Size**: 1024x1024 pixels (standard app store requirement)

- **Corners**: Should be square; iOS/Android will apply rounded corners

## Technical Notes

- Export at 1024x1024 for app stores
- Also export:
  - 180x180 (iOS app icon)
  - 192x192 (Android xxxhdpi)
  - 512x512 (Play Store)
  - 48x48, 96x96, 144x144 (Android various densities)

## Adaptive Icon (Android)

For Android adaptive icons:

- Foreground: The key design
- Background: Solid or gradient indigo
